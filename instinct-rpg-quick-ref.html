Instinct RPG
At-Table Quick Reference · Tier 1
Checks — Gather · Roll · Read Your Dice
Miss (1s)
Remove from pool. GM gains 1 Toll per Miss die — even on success.
Crit (6s)
Explode — roll 1 bonus die. On success, earn 1 Flourish per Crit die.
Beat DC
Success — narrate. Spend Flourishes on card's Crit options.
Below DC
Failure — scene always advances anyway.
Key Resources
Toll
GM resource. Each die showing 1 = +1 Toll, even on success. Spent to make the world react.
Resolve
Temp Hit Dice set up at combat start. Absorbs damage first before health is touched.
Hit Dice
Your health pool — physical dice on the table. Not HP. Enemy dice deal their face values as damage.
Boost N
+N extra dice added to your roll. Discard 1 card = +1 Boost die (declare before rolling).
Card Types
Core
Always-active class rules. Face-up in Active area, never in deck.
Act
Your move. Play on your turn. Cost: 1.
React
Interrupt. Play any time, in response to any action. Cost: 0.
Instinct
A quality driving you right now. Reveal → gain Boost 2.
Background
Your past expertise. Plays on fictional trigger.
Ancestry
Your heritage. Plays when trigger fires in fiction.
Bond
A connection. Find the moment → both gain Boost 1.
Flaw 🔒
Pressure you're under. Lean in or suppress — can't ignore.
Conditions
Exposed
Vulnerable, open. Next attack against target gains Boost 1.
Rattled
Shaken, off-balance. Target must pass a check before acting next beat.
Rooted
Can't reposition. Target cannot Move until cleared.
Marked
Designated target. Cannot be Hidden from whoever placed the Mark.
Hidden
Unseen. Enemies can't target without a check first.
Sundered
Structurally compromised. Allies can consume it for extra effect. (Barbarian special.)
Describe the action. Then find the card.
