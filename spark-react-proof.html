Spark React — Design Lab

Three prototype React cards showing different ways a single d6 resolves — without pool-vs-pool checks. Cards reference the global procedure; they do not re-explain it.

Spark (global rule — primer / GM guide, not on every card): When a card tells you to Spark, roll 1d6 and read that card's outcome bands. No opposing pool, no Tier base dice. The card prints what each band means; moving breakpoints (1–3 vs 1–5 vs three tiers) is how power is budgeted. A natural 1 is not automatically a Miss unless the card says so — Spark is its own procedure, separate from checks and from Luck Check.

Three band templates

A — Three-tier gradient (fail / partial / full) · B — PbtA pick-list (bands gate how many options you take) · C — Binary gate (move the success threshold to tune rarity)
