6 classes · 14 cards · modeled on the Warlock / Barbarian / Rogue / Ranger DNA · June 2026

Monk — new

CoreMonk
Stances
You don't change who you are. You change how you move.
The Three Forms
You hold one Stance — Iron Palm, Wind Step, or Cobra. Its passive is always true while held.
Shift
At the start of your turn you may switch Stance for free. Narrate the change in your footing.
Stances modify how your actions chain — never their damage. Chosen at creation; they do not count as Class Cards.
Monk · CoreNEW
CoreMonk
Momentum
The stillness before the strike. Then everything.
Build
Each action that continues your sequence — Strike into Strike, Move into Strike — adds 1 Momentum die to your pool. Breaking the chain, or being interrupted, empties it.
Release
Spend your Momentum pool as Boost dice on a single action that ends the sequence. The longer the flow, the harder it lands.
Reactions do not break your chain. The fiction defines a 'continued' sequence — the GM adjudicates.

Wizard — new

CoreWizard
Prepared
You already know how this scene begins. You wrote it.
Before the Scene
Set 1 Act card from your hand Prepared, face-up in your Active area. It holds no hand slot while Prepared.
Cast
Play your Prepared card at any moment without drawing it — even out of turn. Then Prepare nothing until the next scene.
Study
When you search your deck, you may immediately Prepare the card you find.
One card Prepared at a time. A Prepared spell may reshape the scene before anyone acts.
Wizard · CoreNEW
CoreWizard
Arcane Burn
Precision has a price. You pay it after.
Overload
Push a spell past its limit to take Arcane Burn and gain Focus now — add Boost 2 to the action, or split one effect across two targets.
The Cost
While Burned, Bolstered washes off you, and your next Check fights a die working against it. Burn clears at scene end.
Consequence is the engine. The GM decides what the Burn disturbs in the world around you.

Cleric — rework

CoreCleric
Prayer
You don't ask for power. You ask for purpose.
Declare — Each Scene
Name your petition aloud — [Verb] the [Noun]. While it stands, every Crit you roll banks one die here, up to your Tier + 1.
Answer — Once Per Scene
Spend the banked dice as Bolstered on allies who serve the petition — split them however you like. Then the prayer is answered; it refreshes next scene.
Changed from self-Boost to ally Bolstered, and capped at Tier + 1 (was Level). Sharpens Cleric as the Bolstered specialist.
Cleric · CoreREWORK
CoreCleric
Absolution
The wound heals. What caused it can too.
Passive
When you heal an ally — restore any Hit Die or clear any damage — they may immediately discard 1 Bane or Status from hand.
The ally chooses whether to discard. Wording tightened; mechanic unchanged.

Bard — rework

CoreBard
Crescendo
I make everyone look good. Then I steal the scene.
Build
Each time an ally succeeds on an action this scene, gain 1 Applause. Once per ally, per round.
Steal the Scene
Spend any amount of Applause as Boost dice on a single action — the later in the scene, the bigger your pool. Spend it all at once.
Applause empties at scene end. This is the missing 'plays best last' engine — proposed in place of Improvise (which can become a normal class card).
Bard · CoreNEW (replaces Improvise)
CoreBard
Showstopper
You make everyone else look good. Or you take the stage.
Once Per Scene — Discard 1
Choose one:
Mimic — copy an ally's action as they perform it. You roll independently.
Lead — an ally copies your action as you perform it. They roll independently.
Flip face-down when used. Refreshes next scene. Unchanged from current design.

Druid — rework

CoreDruid
Beastform
You don't wear another shape. You remember it.
Equip System
You hold 3 Beastform cards, chosen at creation. Equipping one unequips the others → Draw 1. Swap freely between scenes.
While Equipped
Your active Beastform's Kinship passive is always true. Transform freely; make a Check to use a Full Form ability. Beastform blocks Speak entirely.
Added the locked 'blocks Speak' rule for clarity. Otherwise unchanged.
Druid · CoreREWORK
CoreDruid
Grove
Failure is not waste. It is what you tend.
Plant — Session Start
Search your deck for 1 ability card. Place it in the Grove with 1 d6. Shuffle.
Tend — During Play
When any player fails a Check on an ability card, take it into your Grove with 1 d6. Your Grove holds up to your Tier in cards.
Harvest — Each Scene Start
Add 1 d6 to every card in the Grove. Return any card to its owner with all its accumulated d6 as Boost dice.
Clarified capacity (= Tier) and labeled the cadence. Mechanic intact.

Paladin — provisional (identity was blocked)

CorePaladin
Aegis
Your protection doesn't end at your own skin.
Passive
When Resolve is set at the start of a scene, allies within reach each gain 1 Resolve. You decide who, if reach is contested.
Bulwark — React
When an ally would lose Resolve, take that loss onto your own Resolve pool instead. Narrate how you put yourself between them and it.
Replaces Sworn Shield. Guard is gone — rebuilt as a Resolve-manipulation specialist (covers combat AND social). Reach is fictional.
Paladin · CorePROVISIONAL
CorePaladin
Conviction · Defiance
Faith is the action. Doubt is the cost.
Conviction — Passive
While your Oath is held, when you play an ability and narrate how it upholds the Oath, gain 1 Resolve — give it to any ally.
Defiance
When you Enter a scene, you may break your Oath — narrate turning from it. Discard your hand and draw again; your Resolve cannot be shared this scene. The Oath returns only when the GM judges you've earned it back.
Reworked off Guard: Conviction now feeds Resolve (not Draw 1); Defiance's cost is Resolve-sharing, not lost Hit Dice. Oath stays a GM-awarded story card.
