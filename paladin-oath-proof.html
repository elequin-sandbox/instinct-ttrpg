Paladin Oath — Scene Charge Proof

Three Oath Core cards (Devotion · Vengeance · Crown). Player picks one at character creation — mirrors Warlock Patron structure. Dice sit on the card until spent.

Scene Charge — When you Enter a Scene, roll 2d6 onto one Verb and one Noun. That phrase is your priority this scene. Once per scene, if the GM agrees an Action fulfills it, add both dice to that roll and remove them.

Set Your Charge
When you Enter a Scene, roll 2d6. Place one die on a Verb and one on a Noun. Your scene Charge is that phrase — e.g. Protect the Innocent. It is your priority above all else this Scene.
Fulfill Your Charge
Once per Scene, when the GM agrees your Action fulfills your Charge, add both dice from the card to that roll, then remove them.
