Paladin Oath — Scene Vow Proof

Five Oath Core cards + blank templates. Permanent Oath at creation; per-scene Vow (Verb + the + Noun). Optional Break at Enter.

Vow — Roll 2d6 at Enter (GM explains dice placement). Fulfill once/scene when your Action serves your Vow — both dice Boost the roll. Break before rolling: mulligan hand, skip Vow, Oath stays Active.
