Paladin Oath — Scene Stand Proof

Five Oath Core cards + blank templates. Permanent Oath at creation; per-scene Stand (Verb + Noun). Optional Break at Enter.

Stand — Roll 2d6 at Enter. Fulfill once/scene when the primary purpose of your Action serves your Stand. Break before rolling: mulligan hand, skip Stand, Oath stays Active.

Set Your Stand
When you Enter a Scene, roll 2d6. Place one die on a Verb and one on a Noun. Your Stand is that phrase — e.g. Shelter the Frail. It is your priority above all else this Scene.
Fulfill Your Stand
Once per Scene, when the GM agrees your Action's primary purpose fulfills your Stand, add both dice from the card to that roll, then remove them.
Break Your Stand
When you Enter a Scene, before rolling, you may Break your Stand — narrate turning from it. Discard your hand, Draw that many. You do not roll a Stand this scene. Your Oath card stays in the Active area.
