Paladin Oath — Scene Vow Proof

Five Oath Core cards + blank templates. Header tags Core + Oath; ribbon reads Oath of … Per-scene Vow from the 3×2 grid.

Vow — At Scene start, roll 2d6 (one die per row; same column = matching face). Fulfill once per Scene when your Action serves your Vow — Boost 2 from both dice.

Break Your Oath — Describe defying your Vow; place dice into Resolve. GM gains Toll 2 and must spend it against you this scene.
