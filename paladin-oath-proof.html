Paladin Oath — Scene Vow Proof

Five Oath Core cards + blank templates. Permanent Oath at creation; per-scene Vow (Verb + the + Noun). Optional Break at Enter.

Vow — At Enter, roll 2d6 (GM explains dice placement). Fulfill once/scene when your Action serves your Vow in purpose — Boost 2 from both dice. Break before rolling: mulligan hand, skip Vow, Oath stays Active.
