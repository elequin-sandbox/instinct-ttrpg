Miss
Natural 1 — remove immediately. GM gains 1 Toll per Miss (even on success).
Crit
Natural 6 — explodes (roll +1 die; a second 6 counts as Crit but doesn’t explode again).
Flourish
Count dice still showing 6. Each Crit lets you gain one bonus option from the card you played.
Clear
Match your dice to GM dice: equal or higher removes both; lower may reduce GM die by 1 and remove yours. No hidden DC.
Aid
Ally discards a card, narrates how it helps (not the card’s normal effect — GM agrees), +1 die. One ally per check. No self-Aid.
Boost
Extra dice from cards, Aid, or GM reward. Expire at scene end. A 1 on a Boost die is a Miss.
Success
All GM dice cleared. Gain Flourishes for Crits earned.
Partial
Some GM dice remain — not a failure. GM says what landed and what didn’t.
Health & Resources
Hit Dice
Health — physical d6s on the table. Never “HP.” Out of Hit Dice = out of the fight.
Toll
GM currency from Misses. Spent to make the world react.
Scene
Hand discarded at scene end; draw fresh (~5). Party average hand ~2 → expect a transition.
GM teaches when it comes up
Resolve · Threat · Contests · Snap Check — your GM walks the table through these in high-stakes scenes. Ask; that’s normal.

Ancestry

Bond

Connection

Item
In your deck. Many Mill at scene start.
