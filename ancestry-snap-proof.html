Ancestry Snap Check — Proof

All 10 Ancestry deck cards · once per Scene · shared bands 1–3 fail · 4–8 choose 1 · 9+ choose 2. Builder: scripts/build_ancestry_snap.py

Review gate: Approve this proof before Baserow push. React ancestries: Dwarf, Halfling, Half-Orc. Act ancestries: the other seven.

React (3)

Dwarf
AncestryReact
Dwarf
You were carved from stone and stubbornness — and it shows.
Once per Scene, when a blow — physical, social, or emotional — would stagger you, try to hold your ground and make a Snap Check.
Snap Check
1–3The hit lands clean — you show the cost, and the Scene keeps pushing.
4–8Choose 1: Absorb It — take the hit without losing your footing; narrate what you refuse to show · Brace — plant your weight; take Boost 1 on your next check to stay in the fight · Answer Back — meet pressure with a short, grounded reply that shifts the tone
9+Choose 2 from the above.
